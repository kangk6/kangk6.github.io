Embodied AI / Reproduction

OmniDexGrasp 论文复现

OmniDexGrasp Paper Reproduction

论文复现 Paper Reproduction 环境配置 Environment Setup 问题排障 Debugging

这是我围绕具身智能与抓取任务做过的一次论文复现实践。我完成了环境配置，但在实际运行阶段遇到了难以推进的问题， 最终借助 Codex 持续排查与修复，才把复现流程真正往前推进。

This was a paper reproduction practice around embodied intelligence and grasping tasks. I completed the environment setup, but ran into stubborn runtime issues and pushed the workflow forward through repeated debugging with Codex.

这次经历让我更直观地认识到，研究复现不仅是“跑代码”，更是理解依赖关系、实验条件、工程细节与论文设定之间差异的过程。

This experience made me realize that research reproduction is not just about running code, but about understanding dependencies, experimental conditions, engineering details, and the gap between paper settings and real execution.
